About Me

My name is Cameron Lauterbach, owner and operator of the Blue Ridge Canine Academy. I have spent the last 3 years as a canine professional developing my skills with the help of amazing mentors and world renowned teachers.

My Story

As a young kid I loved spending time with my family dogs and taught them many tricks in our backyard. But it wasnâ€™t until we got Nala in 2017 that I started to learn the ins and outs of dog training. While in college we decided to foster a dog, teach it house manners and obedience before finding it a forever home. We brought Nala home and quickly learned around either without barking and lunging. Even though we were able to create a good foundation in obedience, these behaviors led us to find a professional trainer. This is where I decided I wanted to study dog training and psychology so I could help others in a similar situation.

My Journey

A year into my training career, I found my way into the sport dog world. After falling in love with the loyalty and relationships between dog and handler, I decided to get my own German Shepherd and begin training for protection sports - Mondio RIng, and IGP. I spent most of 2019 and 2020 working as the head trainer of a small facility and had the opportunity to work with a large quantity of dogs. This gave me the opportunity to take dogs to a very high level of obedience and was able to change the lives of many families. I worked with many families who came to us as their last hope and I loved seeing their dog transform from a difficult case to a family companion.
